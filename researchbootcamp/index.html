Research & Thesis Bootcamp

Dr. Felix, Associate Professor of Computer Science
This intensive 3-day online bootcamp is designed to transform final-year projects into publishable academic research. Participants will move beyond traditional coursework to master the 'Research Engineering' lifecycle: identifying critical gaps in state-of-the-art literature, formulating rigorous mathematical problem statements, and executing professional-grade experimental pipelines. By the end of the program, students will possess a clear, actionable blueprint to conduct robust experiments, validate results with statistical rigor, and structure a high-impact thesis ready for publication.
