AI Summer Bootcamp

We are hosting a rigorous 5-day AI Bootcamp designed to equip graduating seniors with the tools and knowledge to excel in their AI graduation projects. This intensive program delves into Python for AI, the TensorFlow framework, cutting-edge Vision Transformers for image analysis, and the fascinating world of Large Language Models. By program's end, participants will be well-positioned to tackle their projects with confidence and unlock the full potential of Artificial Intelligence.

AI Day 2024

On March 23, we hosted our inaugural AI Day event at the main Computer Lab. This gathering shed light on the vast potential of Artificial Intelligence and its diverse applications across various fields. Fourth-year undergraduate studetns showcased their innovative projects, with the five most impressive receiving recognition and awards presented by the esteemed Vice Chancellor himself. The event served as a springboard for exciting advancements in AI at the University of Juba.
