AI Courses

Here you'll find list of courses offered at University of Juba covering AI topics.

CCS417 -Deep Learning

Unleash the power of deep learning! Build neural networks, explore cutting-edge AI, and compete in an industry-judged project. Calculus & linear algebra required, Python a bonus.

CCS514 - Computer Vision

Master cutting-edge vision tech: CNNs, Transformers, Generative AI & Diffusion Models. Push the frontier of computer vision by learning task such as object detection and solve real-world challenges.

AI Summer Bootcamp

An intensive, hands-on program to equip participants with the skills to build and deploy data-driven solutions. The program covers data science, machine learning, and deep learning.
