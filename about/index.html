About Us

Established in 2024 by Professor Felix Gonda, the University of Juba's Artificial Intelligence Lab fosters a vibrant intellectual environment. Through collaborative efforts across disciplines and faculties, the Lab fuels groundbreaking research in AI applications for human-robot interaction. We are committed to nurturing the next generation of AI researchers while simultaneously pushing the boundaries of the field. Our dedicated staff provides crucial support, acting as the backbone for our academic and research endeavors. This collaborative ecosystem, encompassing researchers, visiting scholars, and students, fuels innovation and propels the Lab's cutting-edge research agenda.
